Cookie policy

Understand cookie categories, consent options and browser controls.

Last updated: April 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember browsing information and improve user experience.

2. Cookie categories in use

Category Purpose Typical duration
Essential Enable core site functionality and secure navigation. Session or short term
Analytics Help us understand site usage and improve performance. Up to 24 months
Preferences Store user settings for a more personalized experience. Variable

3. Consent management

When visiting the site, you can accept, reject or configure non-essential cookies. You can change your decision later in browser settings.

4. Browser controls

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy.
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions.

5. Policy updates

This page may be updated to reflect legal requirements or technology changes.
